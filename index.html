The Erlyn Design

DURABLE AND SHATTERPROOF

The erlyn is made from shatterproof, borosilicate glass, with a reinforced neck.

100% RECYCLED GLASS

The erlyn is made in the USA from 100% recycled glass, lowering its environmental impact and underscoring its reusability.

DOUBLE-WALL INSULATION

Double-wall insulation ensures a cold drink and a sweat-free bottle, so pour your drink with confidence. You'll also receive a nylon sleeve for added protection.

NON-SLIP BASE

The erlyn has a cork base, preventing table marks and keeping it from sliding.

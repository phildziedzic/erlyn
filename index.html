The Erlyn Design

100% RECYCLED GLASS

The erlyn is made in the USA from 100% recycled glass, lowering its environmental impact and underscoring its reusability.

DURABLE AND SHATTERPROOF

The erlyn is made from shatterproof glass with a reinforced neck and cork base, preventing table marks and cushoining impact if it does take a tumble.

DOUBLE-WALL INSULATION

Double-wall insulation ensures a cold drink and a sweat-free bottle, so pour your drink with confidence.
